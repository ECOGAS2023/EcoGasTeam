    Advantages of Biogas

    Is an eco-friendly biogas is renewable, as well as a clean, source of energy. The generation of this reduces soil and water pollution and produce organic fertilizer. Its a simple and low-cost technology that encourages a circular economy.

    BIO-Chemical Process

    Bio-gas is produce through a bio-chemical process in which certain types of bacteria convert the biological wastes into useful biogas. Since the useful gas originates from biological process, it has been termed as biogas. Methane gas is the main constituent of biogas.
